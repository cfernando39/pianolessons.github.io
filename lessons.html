Lessons

Lessons range from 30 minute lessons, 45 minute lessons and hour lessons, 60 minute lessons. Lesson times would be based on the age and level of the student. For beginner students I generally recommend a 30 minute lesson.

All students are given a FREE TRIAL LESSON. This allows for a proper introductiong between the student and the teacher and gives us a chance to meet and talk about what the student's goals are in taking piano lessons.

In addition, piano lessons are given at my home which presents a warm and welcoming environment which helps ease the stress playing in front of others. Some of the benefits in having lessons in someone else's house is that the students are more likely to behave since it is not their space, fewer distractions which allows for better concentration.
